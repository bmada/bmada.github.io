Let $$ R $$ be a [ring].

## Definition
An **ideal** is a subset $$ I \subseteq R $$ such that $$ (I, +) $$ is a subgroup of the underlying additive group $$ (R, +) $$ and for all $$ x \in I $$ and $$ r \in R $$,

$$ rx \in I \quad xr \in I. $$

Write $$ I \trianglelefteq R $$.
