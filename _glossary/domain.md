## Definition
A **domain** is a [ring][ring] $$ R $$ in which for all $$ r, s \in R $$,
{::comment}
$$ rs = 0 \quad \implies \quad r = 0 \text{ or } s = 0. $$
{:/comment}

$$ rs = 0 $$ implies $$ r = 0 $$ or $$ s = 0 $$.
{: .center .mjx-chtml .MJXc-display}

That is, a domain is a ring without zero divisors.
