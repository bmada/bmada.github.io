Let $$ M $$ be a [differentiable manifold][manifold].

## Definition
The **tangent bundle** $$ TM $$ of the manifold $$ M $$ is the disjoint union of [tangent spaces][tangent space] of $$ M $$,

$$ TM = \bigsqcup_{p \in M} T_p M. $$
