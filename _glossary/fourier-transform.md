Let $$ f \in L^1(\mathbb{R}^n) $$.

<div class="item" markdown="1">
## Definition
The **Fourier transoform** of $$ f $$ is the function $$ \hat{f} $$ defined by

$$ \hat{f}(x) = \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} f(t) e^{-2 \pi i x \cdot t} \, dt $$

for all $$ x \in \mathbb{R}^n $$.
</div>
